All the NTTs you can change (standard):

Important:
You must have the same capitalisation as in this file!

 - Scorch: The flying red monster.
 - WraithGolem: The golem you find at level 30 of Trial of Abyss.
 - ShadeFang: The little corrupted snake coming out of the ground.
 - GrimHowl: The corrupted wolf.
 - ScorchMaster: The scorch you find at the boss level.

What can you do with all of these NTTs?

You can change the update behavior with the "On_NTT_Update{NTT_name}" function.


These are some important things you can use with all NTTs.
Warning: Changing the name may result in your mod breaking and loosing control over the NTT!
 - NTT.name: The NTTs name
 - NTT.health: Change the NTTs health
 - NTT.max_health: Change the NTTs max health
 - NTT.damage: Change the NTTs max damage
 - NTT.defence: Change the NTTs damage
 - NTT.x, NTT.y: Change the NTTs position (These values are x = column, y = row)

The rest are not important or removed later!

Quick hint:
If you want the NTT to have more data just make it yourself!

For now that's it. stay tuned for future updates!
